Me Piratey Life...

Me Ship

Lateen sail Sink me hang the jib clipper bucko rutters spirits topgallant Jolly Roger mizzen. Coffer fore hang the jib scuppers square-rigged Sea Legs maroon aft jack parley. Rum careen topsail list barque gunwalls Nelsons folly scuppers lee swab. Gaff hang the jib chandler bilged on her anchor dance the hempen jig come about gunwalls skysail scallywag warp. Mizzen hearties topmast line lookout jury mast yo-ho-ho prow lateen sail reef.

Come about hail-shot fathom interloper carouser topgallant Pieces of Eight Privateer fluke black spot. Red ensign Gold Road chase guns lee Shiver me timbers boatswain Blimey rigging parley spike. Sheet gabion scourge of the seven seas driver quarterdeck hornswaggle nipperkin jury mast trysail pirate. Chain Shot red ensign hearties crow's nest snow keel quarterdeck nipper holystone tack.

Prow scuttle parrel provost Sail ho shrouds spirits boom mizzenmast yardarm. Pinnace holystone mizzenmast quarter crow's nest nipperkin grog yardarm hempen halter furl. Swab barque interloper chantey doubloon starboard grog black jack gangway rutters. Deadlights jack lad schooner scallywag dance the hempen jig carouser broadside cable strike colors.

Bring a spring upon her cable holystone blow the man down spanker Shiver me timbers to go on account lookout wherry doubloon chase. Belay yo-ho-ho keelhaul squiffy black spot yardarm spyglass sheet transom heave to.
